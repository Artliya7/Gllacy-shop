Our advantages

Gllacy Shop is an online and offline shop selling ice cream of its own production by weight

  • All our ice cream is made in our own production, using modern equipment and time-tested technologies.

  • The purchase of ingredients is made only from proven farms with which we have a long-term cooperation.

  • Cream and milk of the highest quality are used to make ice cream. All ingredients and additives are made from natural raw materials.

  • Delivery of our ice cream is carried out in a special thermal bag, which does not allow the ice cream to melt and allows you to preserve the excellent taste.
